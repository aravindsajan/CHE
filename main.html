Population Growth In India

Advantages of Overpopulation

1. Increased Workforce: A larger population means more people available to work.
2. Larger Consumer Market: A bigger population creates a larger demand for goods and services..
3. Cultural Diversity: Overpopulation can lead to greater cultural diversity.

Disadvantages of Overpopulation:

1. Resource Depletion: Rapid population growth puts pressure on natural resources..
2. Increased Poverty: Overpopulation can strain existing social systems and services..
3. Overburdened Infrastructure: Cities and towns may become overcrowded..
